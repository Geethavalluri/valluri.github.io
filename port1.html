About SUVR & SR College

Commissioner's Message Welcome to SUVR & SR GOVT.POLYTECHNIC FOR WOMEN, ETHAMUKKALA The Govt. Polytechnic for women, Ethamukkala was established in the year 1987. Government have accorded administration sanction for starting Govt. Polytechnic for women in Ethamukkala, Prakasam District for the academic year 1987-88 with three courses i.e., Diploma in ComputerEngineering, Diploma in Electronics and Communication and Diploma in Computer and Commercial Practice with intake of 33 Seats in each course. The AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education), New Delhi, accorded approval for establishment of Govt. Polytechnic for women,Ethamukkala in Prakasam dist., A.P, to conduct the Diploma Courses i.e.,DCME, DECE and DCCP with intake of 33 Seats for each course. The Govt. Polytechnic for women, Ethamukkala is located in theEthamukkala,Kothapatnam Mandal, Prakasam dist. Andhra Pradesh 523280.
